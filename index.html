Letter 01
Where It All Began
In a classroom full of noise and chalk-dusted air, I found something quiet — your eyes, your stare. A warmth I couldn't name, a feeling so new, Something settled in my chest the moment I saw you. We laughed at nothing, copied each other's ways, Tangled in maths problems through slow afternoon haze. Side by side on the dice during the break, Saying everything in silence, no words needed to take. Backstage whispers, moments behind closed doors, Extra class evenings — I always wanted more. The world was loud but somehow you were still, A quiet comfort, warm and familiar — you still are. Exam days revision, your voice soft and low, Pages turning, time passing, neither wanting to go. And games period — oh, those stolen looks, More meaningful than anything written in books. Eye to eye, heartbeat to heartbeat, unhurried, unhurried — That's where it started, in a classroom unworried. That's where I knew, though I hadn't the words, That you were the feeling I'd been waiting towards.

Letter 02
Our Little Dates
A restaurant, soft sofa seats, just you and me, One shake, one straw, and all the world to see. Sipping slowly, fingers close, eyes saying more, The sweetest thing wasn't the shake — it was you I adored. A college parking lot, our own little world, Between the cars and quiet, our stories unfurled. No fancy setting needed, just you standing there, Leaning, laughing, breathing the same afternoon air. Delhi Junction evenings, warm and unhurried, Just two people in love, never worried. Every meetup felt like the first.

Letter 03
Miles Apart, Still So Close
We're far apart but honestly? Can't even tell, Because you call, you text, you cast your little spell! Songs and videos, pics of your cute face, You somehow manage to be my favourite place. You Blinkit-ed me stuff and okay — I melted a little, Who does that?! You do. You adorable riddle. Small fights, small arguments, yes they happen too, But honestly even those feel cute coming from you. Long nonsense talks all day! My favourite thing. Long cheesy talks? I let you say everything. "Babies babies babies" — yes that's fully you, And somehow I love every baby thing you do. You became my best friend when I needed a laugh, My mother when I was lost on the wrong path, My wife who just pampers me without being asked — How did I get so lucky? I'm genuinely baffled. God just looked at me one day and thought "This one deserves something special, something that can't be bought." So he sent me you — my real angel, my gem, Out of everyone in the world, I got you BABE ❤️

Letter 04
Our Future, Our Promise
We're not perfect, and that's perfectly fine, Every great love takes patience, yours and mine. With time we'll grow, with trust we'll stand tall, With determination we'll rise every time we fall. Every dream we whispered, every plan we made, Every little mission — none of it will fade. We'll chase it all together, side by side, With your strength and my heart, nothing we can't ride. And listen — you know me, I forget things, I do, Dates, details, sometimes even what I said to you. 😅 I'm sorry for the moments I let you down, For the times I turned your smile into a frown. It was never on purpose, never from the heart, Hurting you was never, ever my part. But love — oh love — what I feel for you, Goes deeper than words, deeper than I ever knew. It's in the way I think of you first thing each day, In how I'd cross any distance just to find your way. It's in the way I want to be better, do more, Give you everything I have and then search for more. You deserve the world and I'll spend my whole life, Making sure you feel it — through every joy, every strife. I promise to stand with you in your highs and lows, In your lefts and your rights, wherever life goes. In your 3am storms and your brightest noon, I'll be your constant — your sun, your moon. Whenever you need me, I'll already be there, Loving you in every way, beyond compare.
